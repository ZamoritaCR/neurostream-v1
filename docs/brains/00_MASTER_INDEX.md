**File:** `BRAIN_01_ADHD_DEEP_RESEARCH.md`

### BRAIN #3: ADD/Inattentive Research
**File:** `BRAIN_03_ADD_RESEARCH.md`
**Lines:** 618 | **Citations:** 55+

- ADHD-PI (inattentive) often missed/misdiagnosed

### BRAIN #6: DBT/CBT Integration
**File:** `BRAIN_06_DBT_CBT.md`
**Lines:** 768 | **Citations:** 55+

**Core Skills for App:**
- **TIPP:** Temperature, Intense exercise, Paced breathing, Progressive relaxation
- **STOP:** Stop, Take step back, Observe, Proceed mindfully
- **ACCEPTS:** Activities, Contributing, Comparisons, Emotions, Push away, Thoughts, Sensation
- **Opposite Action:** Act counter to emotional urge
- **Radical Acceptance:** Accept reality without fighting it
- **Cognitive Reappraisal:** Reframe situation to modify emotional impact

**New Research Findings:**
- Digital reappraisal training (ReApp) reduces anger/disgust
- TEA intervention: Anxiety (Œ≤ = ‚àí1.46), maintained at 30-day follow-up
- Reappraisal most effective for those with "additional vulnerabilities" (ADHD users!)
- CBT techniques: Guided discovery, thought records, behavioral experiments
- MHapps show medium effect size for emotion regulation

**Application:** Therapeutic framework for technique library
